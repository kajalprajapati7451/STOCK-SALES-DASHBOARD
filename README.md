# 📊 Inventory & Sales Dashboard (Power BI + Excel)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an **Inventory & Sales Analytics Dashboard** built using **Microsoft Excel and Power BI**.
The dashboard provides insights into sales performance, product contribution, customer purchasing patterns, and state-wise sales distribution.

The objective of this project is to transform raw sales data into **interactive visual insights** that can help businesses make better decisions.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

### 1️⃣ Sales Overview Analysis

This page provides a high-level overview of business performance.

Key Insights:

* Monthly Sales Trend
* Top Performing Products
* Top 5 Customers by Revenue
* State-wise Sales Distribution
* KPI Cards (Total Sales, Total Quantity, Total Products, Total Customers)

---

### 2️⃣ Product Performance Analysis

This section focuses on understanding product-level performance.

Key Insights:

* Product Revenue Share
* Product Sales by State
* Product Quantity vs Price
* Product Demand vs Price Impact
* Average Product Price

---

### 3️⃣ Customer & State Sales Analysis

This page analyzes customer behavior and regional sales performance.

Key Insights:

* Customer Average Purchase Rate
* Customer Purchase Quantity
* Customer Revenue Contribution
* Customer vs Product Sales Matrix
* Total Orders and Total States

---
